## III. Two Futures (8 min)

### A. At Current Speed (~3 min)

What happens with no additional funding or support — just the people in this room and the people already involved.

- The working group continues coding cases, slowly
- The Pro app continues improving based on user feedback
- The Personal app gets built, eventually — nights and weekends
- IRR data accumulates — but slowly, one case at a time
- Era 1 happens. It just takes years longer than it needs to.
- Meanwhile the field continues producing the same results with the same implicit model

*"Everything I showed you today will keep moving. The question is whether it moves fast enough to matter."*

### B. With Support (~5 min)

What becomes possible with funding, institutional backing, or even just more people.

**Research acceleration:**
- Dedicated coders → IRR dataset grows 10x faster
- Sufficient ground truth → AI coding becomes reliable
- Era 2 arrives: AI finds patterns humans couldn't see
- Cross-site studies become possible — same data model, different practitioners, comparable results

**Training transformation:**
- Objective competency testing — compare trainee coding to ground truth
- Curriculum based on measurable outcomes, not just supervision hours
- A trainee in Brazil and a trainee in Alaska evaluate the same case and get comparable results

**Clinical tools at scale:**
- Personal app as standard intake — every new client gets a structured family assessment before session one
- AI-assisted coding in real-time during sessions
- Aggregate data across practitioners — for the first time, population-level patterns in family emotional process

**The shift:**
- Right now, every clinician is an island. Their observations die with their caseload.
- With shared data and shared variables, observations accumulate. Knowledge compounds.
- That's what measurement does. It lets the work of one generation build on the last.

## IV. The Next Generation (4 min)

Deliver on Pres 3's promise: "More on this at day's end."

### You don't have to be an engineer.

This work needs people at every level, and most of the levels are clinical, not technical.

**Use the tools:**
- The Pro app is available now. Draw the family. Track the timeline. See what you've been missing.
- The Personal app is coming. Your clients will be able to generate their own family assessment before they walk in.

**Join the seminar:**
- Monthly Zoom. Clinicians using the app, coding cases, sharpening each other's thinking.
- Not a lecture series. A working group.

**Code cases:**
- The IRR study needs trained coders. That's clinicians.
- You listen to a case, you code it using SARF, your coding gets compared to others.
- This is the ground truth pipeline. Humans set the standard. You are the humans.

**Bring your observations:**
- You see patterns in your practice that no one else sees. Right now those observations exist in your head and nowhere else.
- SARF gives you a way to record them so they can be tested.
